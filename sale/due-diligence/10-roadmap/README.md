# 10 — Roadmap

## Purpose

Plausible 12-month growth path a buyer with typical SaaS resources could execute after transfer. Explicitly framed as a planning tool, not a forecast.

## Files that will be added

- `growth-roadmap.md` — 12-month plan in phases: Handover, Alpha, Beta, Referral Loop, Repeatable Acquisition, Retention, Category Decision (mirrors `sale/flippa/growth-roadmap.md`)
- `first-30-days.md` — critical first-month tasks: security hardening, Stripe integration, wedge decision, alpha enrolment
- `first-90-days.md` — second-quarter tasks: content marketing, first paid ads test, NPS baseline
- `cost-model-year-1.md` — hosting, marketing, contractors, legal budgets across four quarters
- `hire-plan.md` — recommended sequence of hires as revenue grows (first: part-time SDR, month 4; first FT engineer or marketer, month 12)
- `optional-scaling-paths.md` — Postgres migration, Redis session store, HIPAA compliance project, dedicated Brevo IP

## NDA classification

**Share after NDA.** The roadmap contains assumptions about market strategy that are not appropriate for public distribution. Provided upon signed NDA.

## Reader notes

- **This is not a forecast.** Numbers, milestones, and hire timings are illustrative. Actual outcomes depend on buyer execution, market conditions, and factors outside the seller's control.
- The roadmap assumes: ≥ 20 hours/week of buyer focus for first six months, ~40 hours/month engineering capacity, ~$15,000 marketing budget across year 1, single-wedge focus. If any of these assumptions fail, timelines must be extended.
- The roadmap treats revenue as an outcome of well-executed sales activity, not a target to hit at any cost. Buyers evaluating this asset for passive income should stop at this section.
