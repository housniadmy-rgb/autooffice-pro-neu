# 11 — Buyer FAQ

## Purpose

Anticipated buyer questions, answered honestly. Reduces the volume of Flippa messaging back-and-forth and lets the buyer self-serve on standard concerns.

## Files that will be added

- `buyer-faq.md` — canonical 32-question FAQ in five categories: Business & Financials (7), Technical (10), Product (5), Transfer & Post-Sale (6), Legal & Risk (4). Mirrors `sale/flippa/buyer-faq.md`.
- `faq-additions-log.md` — running log of new questions received from prospective buyers via Flippa messaging, batched into the main FAQ for future revisions
- `common-objections.md` — how the seller responds to typical objections (asking price too high, no test suite, ephemeral hosting, no HIPAA, no marketing)

## NDA classification

**Public.** The FAQ is designed to reduce friction at the first buyer contact and is shared without NDA. `common-objections.md` is public too — the seller's positioning on standard concerns is transparent.

## Reader notes

- Read the FAQ before scheduling a call. Nine out of ten first-round questions are covered there.
- If a question is not in the FAQ, ask via Flippa messaging. Common new questions are added to `faq-additions-log.md` and folded into the master FAQ during the next document revision.
- The seller does not use the FAQ to close deals or apply pressure. It exists to save both sides time.
