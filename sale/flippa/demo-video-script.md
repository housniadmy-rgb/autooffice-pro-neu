# PraxisOnline24 — 2-Minute Demo Video Script

> Two minutes = 260 spoken words at a natural pace of 130 wpm. This script is timed to that budget. Total on-screen time: 120 seconds including a title card and an outro. Words in **bold** hit at cadence beats — record with slight emphasis.

---

## Production notes

- **Voiceover only** — no talking head. Cleaner audio, easier to re-record.
- **Screen recording:** 1920 × 1080, 30 fps. QuickTime → New Screen Recording works.
- **Cursor visible.** Use a subtle click-highlight extension (Cursor Highlighter for Chrome, or macOS's built-in).
- **No transitions.** Simple cuts. Trust the story.
- **Background music:** low, instrumental. Use royalty-free (Uppbeat, Epidemic Sound). Keep music under -18 dB so voice is clear.
- **End card:** static, 3 seconds. Shows domain, asking price, "Serious offers via Flippa".
- **Captions:** burn in permanent English subtitles. International buyers watch with sound off.

## What NOT to say in the video

- Do **not** cite MRR, ARR, customer count, growth rate — there are none.
- Do **not** claim "GDPR compliant" or "HIPAA ready" — say "privacy-conscious schema".
- Do **not** show DevTools, source code, or your seller identity.
- Do **not** show any real practitioner's or patient's data.
- Do **not** imply a specific ROI. Buyers should form their own view.

## Recording checklist

- [ ] Microphone quality check (Blue Yeti or equivalent; USB gaming headsets sound cheap).
- [ ] Screen recording done at 1920 × 1080, 30 fps, no browser bookmarks visible.
- [ ] Test upload to Flippa video slot; verify aspect and audio survive their transcode.
- [ ] Prepare captions file (.srt) so hearing-impaired viewers can follow.
- [ ] Export a compressed MP4 (H.264, ~5 Mbps bitrate) for the main version.
- [ ] Keep a lossless ProRes 422 master in the `sale/flippa/video/` folder for future re-edits.
